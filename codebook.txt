Overview
===========

The variables are a subset of average value for all mean and standard deviation samples for training and test for UCI HAR Dataset. 

All veriable names include the following parts:
t' prefix denotes time -- values captured at 50hz rate
'f' prefix denotes Finally a Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) applied to signals

'mean' Mean value
'std' Standard deviation
